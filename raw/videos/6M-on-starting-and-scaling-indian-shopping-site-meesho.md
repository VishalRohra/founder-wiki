# On starting and scaling Indian shopping site Meesho 

[Vidit Aatrey](https://twitter.com/viditaatrey) is cofounder and CEO of [Meesho](https://meesho.com/). Meesho is a
platform in India that allows people to resell products using their social networks. They were in the Summer 2016 batch
of YC and you can check them out at [Meesho.com](https://meesho.com/).

[Adora Cheung](https://twitter.com/nolimits) is a Partner at YC. Before working at YC she cofounded Homejoy.

You can find Vidit on Twitter [@viditaatrey](https://twitter.com/viditaatrey) and Adora is
[@nolimits](https://twitter.com/nolimits).
