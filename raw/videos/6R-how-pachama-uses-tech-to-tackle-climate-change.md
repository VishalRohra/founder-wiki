# How Pachama uses tech to tackle climate change

Diego Saez Gil is the founder of [Pachama](http://www.pachama.com/) (YC W19). Pachama is building a marketplace where
companies can support carbon offset projects.

He talks to Craig Cannon and YC partner Gustaf Alströmer about how Pachama uses technology to help solve climate change.
