# Do technical founders need a business co-founder?

Whether or not you need a co-founder is always a hotly debated topic — but going one level deeper, founders often wonder
what **kind** of co-founder is best.

In a prior video Michael and I explained [*why ****every**** software company should have a technical
co-founder*](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/KO-does-your-tech-startup-really-need-a-technical-co-founder-yes). But
it’s also common for technical founders to be told by investors and advisors that they would benefit from a co-founder
with non-technical expertise in things like business or marketing.

Are they correct? In this episode of [*Dalton &
Michael*](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/carousel/Dalton%20&%20Michael) we dive into exactly that question.

- Dalton
