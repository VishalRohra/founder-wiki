# On starting and scaling construction startup PlanGrid

[Tracy Young](https://twitter.com/Tracy_Young) is a cofounder and the CEO of [PlanGrid](https://www.plangrid.com/).
PlanGrid makes mobile construction productivity software. They were acquired by Autodesk in 2018 and were part of YC’s
Winter 2012 batch.

[Kat Manalac](https://twitter.com/KatManalac) is a Partner at YC.

You can find Tracy on Twitter [@Tracy\_Young](https://twitter.com/Tracy_Young) and Kat is
[@KatManalac](https://twitter.com/KatManalac).
