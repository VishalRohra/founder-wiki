# Garry's Channel: Should you be the CEO?

We were pitching A16z, with Marc Andreessen and Ben Horowitz. They asked us who was CEO. We said both. It was the wrong
answer.

This video is about why people try to do shared leadership, and how you can tell if you should be CEO or not. It might
seem like its not important... but it is.
