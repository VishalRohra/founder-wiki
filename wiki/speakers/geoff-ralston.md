# Geoff Ralston

Geoff Ralston is a partner at Y Combinator. He interviewed the Embark Trucks founding team in late 2015 and stayed in close contact with them through their IPO. His public writing in the YC canon focuses on evaluating young founders and hard tech companies [1].

## Key Perspectives

Ralston demonstrates how YC evaluates very young founders. When interviewing the 19-20 year old Embark team, his initial concern was whether they were "merely playing with golf carts and having a good time while never planning to leave school" [1]. What changed his mind: "Their thinking was startlingly clear and robust. Their answers were direct and to the point. They had a plan and were serious and, by the way, they'd already built the first self-driving vehicle in Canada" [1].

His conclusion: "Those qualities make age irrelevant" [1]. This aligns with the broader YC stance on [[Starting Young]] -- credentials matter less than demonstrated building and clarity of thought.
