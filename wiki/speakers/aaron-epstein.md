# Aaron Epstein

Aaron Epstein is a founder of [Creative Market](https://creativemarket.com/) who wrote about his experience learning management as a first-time founder [1].

## Worldview

Epstein approaches [[Management|management]] from the perspective of a founder who had to learn on the job. His advice is tactical and specific, covering the full spectrum from delegation to 1-on-1s to career pathing. He frames the management challenge as inseparable from the product challenge: "you have to create both a product that people love and a company where people want to work at the same time" [1].

## Key Principles

- **People leave managers, not companies**: The quality of management directly determines retention [1]
- **Hire slow, fire fast**: The discipline few have but everyone needs [1]
- **Hire for hunger over skills**: Passion for the mission drives long-term impact [1]
- **Deflect credit, absorb blame**: Use "I" for failures, specific names for successes [1]
- **Manage for the employee**: The best management style "depends on the employee" [1]
- **Ease the maker-to-manager transition**: Outstanding ICs may take on 150% workload during transition, producing less than 100% output in each area [1]
