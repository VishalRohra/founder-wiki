# Conversion Rates

Conversion rate optimization is the process of increasing the percentage of users who take a desired action -- signing up, purchasing, completing onboarding, or any other key step in the user journey. [[Gustaf Alströmer]], YC partner and former growth lead at Airbnb, provides a systematic framework for identifying and fixing conversion bottlenecks [1].

## The Funnel Mindset

Every user interaction is a funnel with drop-off at each step. The goal is not to optimize every step simultaneously but to identify the step with the largest absolute drop-off and focus there [1]. A 5% improvement at a bottleneck that 100,000 users pass through is worth more than a 50% improvement at a step only 100 users reach.

## Measurement Before Optimization

Alströmer emphasizes that most startups try to optimize conversion before they can measure it accurately. The prerequisites [1]:
- Clear definition of each step in the funnel
- Reliable tracking of users through each step
- Enough volume to detect meaningful changes (statistical significance)

Without these, optimization efforts are guesswork.
